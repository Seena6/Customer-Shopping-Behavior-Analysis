# Key Insights

Here are some of the most meaningful findings from the analysis:

Adults and Young Adults contribute the highest share of total revenue.

Customers with more than 5 previous purchases show a strong likelihood of being subscribers.

Express shipping users tend to spend more than standard shipping users.

Certain products rely heavily on discounts to drive purchases, while others perform well without them.

Top-selling products often align with those having higher review ratings.

Categories with strong repeat buying behavior stand out as core revenue drivers.

# Recommendations

Based on the analysis, a few strategies can help improve customer engagement and revenue:

Strengthen loyalty programs to convert returning buyers into long-term loyal customers.

Review the discount strategy, especially for items that don‚Äôt require heavy promotions.

Highlight top-rated products in campaigns to boost visibility and trust.

Promote express shipping, especially during peak seasons, as high-value customers prefer it.

Use targeted marketing for age groups and customer segments with higher revenue potential.
